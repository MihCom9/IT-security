How To Stay Secure Online

  • Settup Strong Password

    Always use a strong password for important websites and for your email. The requirement for a strong password is:

    • The password has to be at least 12 characters long.
    • It needs to have at least one number, small letter, big letter and a special character

    Your password must not contain any personal data(such as your name, banking details or age). Like this even if your password gets stollen a hacker wouldn't know anything for you from it.

  • Use Different Unique passwords

    For every site which has your personal data use a different and unique password. And never use the same password for less important sites. In this way if a hacker gets a password for a less important site, he couldn't use it to get your personal data.

    If you are having a hard time making unique passwords you can use an online site or a password manager

  • Use Multy Factor Authentication

    Multifactor authentication (MFA) is an authentication method that asks users to provide two or more verification methods to access an online account. Some of the methods are:

    • A one time password which is being sent to the user's email
    • The password or fingerprint for the device
    • Answer to personal questions

    It's very useful because even if a hacker gets your password, he still will need to go through the authentication. Which might prevent him from stealing anything
